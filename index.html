Crazy about chameleons. They have googly eyes, colour-changing skin and tongues twice as long as their bodies! Who needs to search for aliens when there are weird creatures like chameleons roaming around?

2023 Quaide Rumble

COLOUR ME MOODY. Chameleons are masters of camouflage, but did you know that they don’t just change colour to hide from predators? Sometimes they do it when they’re moody, and sometimes they do it in response to temperature or light. When chameleons are angry, stressed or scared, for example, their colours get really dark. When they’re excited, they’re more likely to display bright greens, reds and blues. They also tend to go dark when they’re chilly, as dark colours absorb more heat and will keep them warm.
